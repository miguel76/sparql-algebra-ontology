SPARQL Algebra Ontology

Namespace URI: https://w3id.org/sparql-algebra-ontology#
Preferred Prefix: spa:
Version Info: Revision: 1.1.0
Authors: Miguel Ceriani.

An RDF schema used to represent SPARQL algebra as RDF.

Classes: Aggregation, 1-Arity Operation, BGP, 0-Arity Operation, Bound aggregate, Distinct, expression, Extend, Filter, Graph, Group, Operation, 2-Arity Operation, N-Arity Operation, OrderBy, Project, Reduced, Slice, Table, ToList, ToMultiset.

Object Properties: assigned expression, bound aggregate, bound aggregate expression, bound aggregate variable, bound variable, filter expression, grouping expression, left subOperation, subOperation, projected variable, right subOperation, tuple.

Datatype Properties: .
